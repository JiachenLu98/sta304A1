Abstraction

My dataset is about Air conditioning information for the apartment in Toronto. We use Licensing/Standards (2020)

Data Introduction

We can conclude that most of the apartment in Toronto does not have air-conditioning. Rest of them are Central Air or Individual Unit. They are categorial varible and are nearly equal in amount.

Reference

Licensing/Standards, Municipal. 2020. “Apartment Building Registration.” Journal of Open Source Software 4 (43): 1686. https://doi.org/10.21105/joss.01686.
